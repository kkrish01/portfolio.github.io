PROJECTS

vnike

vnike is a PHP-based social media platform that allows users to create accounts, connect with others, send voice requests, engage in live chat, form groups, and share posts. Users can interact with posts by liking, rating, and commenting. vnike is my first startup, aiming to offer a user-friendly space for social networking and personal connections.

musicverse

musicverse is a college project dedicated to music lovers. Here users can create accounts, stream music for free, like and save their favorite tracks, and share their downloaded music with the community.

bookaro

Bookaro, my second college project, was developed to solve a common problem. It's a PHP-based platform where students can create accounts and post their old books for sale at lower prices. Other students looking to purchase books can find affordable deals.

SSR Courier

SSR Courier, my internship project, is a Laravel-based system designed for efficient inward and outward tracking of goods between branches. The project includes code optimization, integration of a GST report module, and the addition of a new ledger module. It also offers order tracking and the capability to download PDF reports.

Absolute Fit

Absolute Fit is a gym management project I enhanced during my internship. I redeveloped the follow-up system, optimized the code, and resolved all existing bugs. This PHP-based software offers a powerful solution for health and fitness management, empowering gym administrators and trainers to efficiently track and manage client progress.

Hawkeye

Hawkeye is a live order tracking software built on the Laravel framework. I've worked on building and optimizing trip management features within this software. Hawkeye offers real-time tracking capabilities for efficient order management, making it a valuable tool for logistics and delivery businesses.
